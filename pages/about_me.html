About Me

I recently graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and am excited to begin my career as a software engineer. My interest in programming started in early 2022, focusing on Python and web development. Later that year, I committed to deepening my skills by pursuing a degree in computer science, which has provided me with a strong foundation in both backend and frontend development.

Throughout my studies, I worked on several hands-on projects that are available on my GitHub, and I'm in the process of building a portfolio to showcase them. Now that I've graduated, I'm ready to bring my passion for coding and problem-solving to a full-time role in software engineering, eager to contribute to a growing team and continue learning along the way.
